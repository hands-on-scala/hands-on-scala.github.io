Our first Scala dojo

published on May 20, 2016
What is a coding dojo? In short, a coding dojo is an event where a group of programmers practice their craft in an organized way, focusing on a single coding problem. A dojo usually lasts a couple of hours, depending on the style and the task to be solved. For more details see CodingDojo.org or Joe Wright’s blog post about coding dojos.
